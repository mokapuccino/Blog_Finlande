Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences

Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences which is called SeAMK (because it's Seinäjoen ammattikorkeakoulu in finnish) is a University founded in 1995. There is currently 4700 students. The university has its own website : SeAMK.fi
