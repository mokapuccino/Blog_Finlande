How to get there

To go to Finland, we will take the plane. Three companies are fighting for the French-Finnish air market, Finnair, Easyjet and Blue1. Thomas and Emma will leave from Paris Beauvais airport to arrive at Helsinki airport by Finnair. For Killian and Ines, the departure will be from Charles de Gaulles airport in Paris to arrive in Helsinki. .
