How to get there

To go to Finland, we will take the plane. To get to the airport we decided to take the train. Ines and Killian will leave from Angers-Saint-Laud on February 10th to go to Paris Charles de Gaulle 2 airport while Thomas and myself (Emma) will fly from Paris Beauvais airport on February 11th. Personally, I will go there on February 10th by taking a train from Saint-Nazaire to Paris-Montparnasse and then one or more shuttles to Beauvais where I will spend the night with friends before going to the airport on the 11th. We will all arrive at Helsinki Vantaa airport, Killian and Ines on February 10th and Thomas and I on the 11th.
